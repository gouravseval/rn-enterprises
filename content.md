## 4. SERVICES PAGE (The Technical Deep Dive)

### A. CNC Laser & Plasma Cutting
* **Description:** We offer high-speed cutting for Stainless Steel, Mild Steel, and Aluminum.
* **Technical Specs:** Cutting accuracy up to 0.05mm; handling sheets up to 20mm thickness.
* **Use Case:** Complex industrial parts, decorative panels, and automotive components.

### B. Structural Steel Fabrication
* **Description:** Heavy-duty fabrication for the construction and infrastructure sectors.
* **Expertise:** Industrial sheds, PEB (Pre-Engineered Buildings), mezzanines, and staircases.
* **Standard:** Built to withstand high-stress environments and extreme weather.

### C. Precision Welding (TIG/MIG)
* **Description:** Specialized welding services for high-strength joints and aesthetic finishes.
* **Materials:** Specialized in SS 304/316, Aluminum alloys, and carbon steel.
* **Finishing:** Grinding and polishing for seamless, "ready-to-show" joints.

### D. Custom Sheet Metal Forming
* **Description:** Bending, punching, and rolling to create complex 3D shapes from flat sheets.
* **Machinery:** Multi-axis CNC Press Brakes for perfect angles every time.
